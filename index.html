Are you looking to relocate to the Sunshine State? Maybe you're not sure where to start. Our experienced real estate professionals at Carlisle & Hartranft LLC will match you with top agents and lenders in your dream destination, and help you with the first crucial steps in your dream home purchase, all at no cost to you!

If you are serious about relocating to Florida, contact us using the form directly above, and continue reading to learn more about the services we offer.

We will not hound you to make a deal if you are not ready - these are no cost & no pressure services designed to make your life easier and help you to avoid some of the common pitfalls of buying and selling property. Don't go at it alone! Let our experience help you do this the right way.

Step 1 in your journey is to find your dream destination! Maybe you already have a place in mind or maybe you are still narrowing down your options.

If you are still having trouble choosing, we recommend you check out our friends at Find Your Florida. Their interactive map in our opinion is the best tool for virtually exploring the many wonderful destinations the Sunshine State can/has to offer.

Step 2 - this one is a big timesaver and will save you some headaches down the road - gather the following crucial documents and keep them available to you.

  • Tax Returns
  • Bank Statements
  • Proof of Employment

Step 3 is simple - reach out to one of our experienced & licensed professionals at Carlisle & Hartranft by using the intake form located at the top of this page.

Explain to us your unique situation: where are you coming from, where are you looking to go, what is your price range, and any other information you feel we need to know to help guide you better. You need to deal with the right person - let us help you accomplish that!

You may also want to skim our FAQ section to help answer some of your basic starting questions.

Now that we're in contact, we can start to help you make things happen.

Step 4 is to match you with a top lender in the area of your choice. Our company is able to refer some of the best lenders and agents in a variety of the most popular destinations in Florida, and we always use local lenders because they have a better grasp of the market than outside lenders.
